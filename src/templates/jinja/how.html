How to Play

1

Your time starts now

Every Friday at 3pin you will be sent an email and the clock will start ticking...

2

Take a look

Click the link in the email to be taken to that week's film screenshot. We generously give you 5 1/2 hours to submit your answer... go go go!

3

Race against the clock

The clock will drop 1 point per second and there are a maximum of 20,000 points available from the 3pm email being sent. The faster you answer, the higher your score.

4

Give us a clue

If you don't recognise the screenshot you can request up to 3 clues. For every clue requested, or any incorrect answers submitted, you'll instantly drop 2,000 points.

5

What are you waiting for

The game is divided Up into seasons of 12 weeks, but you can join in anytime.

6

Winner winner

There's an ongoing leaderimard and of course, some prizes available for the winner of each season. There will be rewards for fastest answer and consistency of play.
